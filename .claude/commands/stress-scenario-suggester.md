Research current financial market developments and suggest stress scenarios for market risk testing.

Launch 5 parallel research agents to investigate:
1. Geopolitical risks (conflicts, sanctions, political instability)
2. Macroeconomic risks (monetary policy, inflation, recession)
3. Market structure risks (leverage, liquidity, concentration)
4. Climate & ESG risks (physical, transition, regulatory)
5. Technology & sector risks (AI disruption, cyber, sector vulnerabilities)

Each agent should use WebSearch to find current developments and suggest 2-3 stress scenarios.

After all agents complete, synthesize findings into:
- Executive summary with top 3-5 priority scenarios
- Detailed findings by domain
- Prioritization matrix (urgency vs severity)
- Recommended next steps for pillar-stress-generator

Output a comprehensive report that Market Risk can review to decide which scenarios to develop.
